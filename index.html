The Vision of Future

The vision for the future of a New Bangladesh is to foster innovation, sustainability, and progress through technological advancements, economic growth, and social development. It aims to build a prosperous, inclusive, and resilient nation with a focus on green energy, digital transformation, and improving the quality of life for all citizens.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Green energy refers to energy derived from renewable and environmentally friendly sources

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure refers to the advanced systems, facilities, to support the efficient functioning of a society.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/25

Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh is actively advancing its space capabilities with plans to launch a second satellite, the Bangladesh Earth Observatory Satellite, within the next two to three years. This satellite aims to enhance the country's ability to monitor natural disasters, manage agricultural resources, and assess environmental changes

Date: 05/01/25

Time: 10.25 PM

The initiative follows the successful deployment of Bangladeshs first geostationary communications and broadcasting satellite, Bangabandhu-1, which was launched on May 12, 2018, from the Kennedy Space Center in the USA. Manufactured by Thales Alenia Space, Bangabandhu-1 marked Bangladesh's entry as the 57th country to independently operate a satellite in space.
